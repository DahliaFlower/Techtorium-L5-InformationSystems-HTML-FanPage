Baby Pics

Taylor Alison Swift was born on 13th December 1989. Her parents are Scott & Andrea Swift. Her dad is a Financial Advisor and her Mother a marketing
executive. They chose the name Taylor because it was gender neutral and would help her build a business later in life.
Taylor has a younger brother Austin who is an actor and producer.

Her early life was spent on a Christmas Tree farm in Pennsylvania. She attend a couple of elementary schools and developed interests in Horse Riding
and theatre. It wasn't until junior high that Taylor became passionate about country music. At age 12 she learnt the guitar and fell in love with writing music.

Awards

Taylor Swift has become one of the most successful women in American Music history.In her career, she has been nominated for over 500 awards and won
324 of those nominations. Her achievements include 11 Grammies, eight Brit Awards, 34 American Music, 25 Billboard and 25 Country Music Awards.
Her accolades include: Best Artist, Best Entertainer, Best Song, Best Music video, Best Female Artist and many many more.
